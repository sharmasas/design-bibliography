A REPOSITORY FOR DIGITAL EXPERIMENTS

SAS_LABS is a home for digital projects I plan to develop to learn more about software as a medium for expressing and executing ideas. I've been interested in both art and technology for as long as I can remember: this site serves as my canvas and lab as I try to make sense of digital media. A portfolio of my other work -- academic, professional, and personal -- can be found at www.sanasharma.com, while my Tumblr, www.sharmasas.tumblr.com, serves as a collection of in-process artwork and essays.
