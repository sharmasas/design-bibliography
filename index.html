A REPOSITORY FOR DIGITAL EXPERIMENTS

SAS_LABS is a home for digital projects I plan to develop to learn more about software as a medium for expressing ideas. I have been interested in both art and technology for as long as I can remember, and along the way have studied physics, art, and architecture on the path to becoming a UX Designer. This site serves as my canvas and lab as I tinker and learn more about digital media. A portfolio of my other work -- academic, professional, and personal -- can be found at www.sanasharma.com, while my Tumblr, www.sharmasas.tumblr.com, serves as a collection of in-process artwork and essays.
